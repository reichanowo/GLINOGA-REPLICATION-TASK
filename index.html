Ensuring Peaceful and Fair Elections in 2025

ICT Hands-on Activity on HTML Texts

Introduction

The 2025 midterm elections are approaching, and maintaining a peaceful and fair environment is critical. Every effort must be made to promote ethical practices during this time. The filing period for certificates of candidacy ended on the 30th of October 2023, marking the beginning of this crucial period leading up to the elections.

Promoting Fair Campaigning

The Commission on Elections (Comelec), despite challenges, can work with civic organizations and government agencies to remove campaign materials placed in public spaces, such as trees, lampposts, and school walls. This action can be taken even before the official campaign period to prevent violations of environmental laws.

Preventing Election Violence

The PNP has committed to ensuring safety during the elections. With over 18,280 elective positions at stake, the task is substantial. However, political violence, including intimidation and harassment, must be stopped. Politicians must reject violence and instead focus on ethical campaigning
